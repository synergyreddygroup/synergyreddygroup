Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Synergy Reddy Group (SRG) and its subsidiaries—Canies Academy, Bitoramart, Infinited Art, and Agrizap—respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platforms and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, device type, IP address, access time, and pages visited.
  • Transaction Data: Purchases, billing information, and delivery address (where applicable).
  • Educational Information: For academic services on Canies Academy (e.g., student names, grades, quiz performance).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To communicate updates and promotional offers
  • To personalize your user experience
  • To fulfill legal or regulatory obligations

4. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures—including encryption, secure databases, and access control—to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share information with third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors or delivery partners) strictly for operational purposes, with confidentiality agreements in place.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics tools to enhance your browsing experience and analyze user behavior. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our websites may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party websites.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Educational data collected via schools is managed securely with appropriate permissions.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data collection (where applicable)
  • Request data portability or restriction of processing

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
