Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common queries about our services, pricing, and support across all Synergy Reddy Group brands.

1What is Synergy Reddy Group?

Synergy Reddy Group is a multi-venture organization involved in education, e-commerce, creative design, and agri-delivery. Our subsidiaries include Canies Academy, Bitoramart, Infinited Art, Refliator, and Agrizap.

2Are all services offered online?

Yes, all our platforms are accessible online. Canies Academy offers courses and workshops digitally, Bitoramart and Agrizap take online orders, and our design services are delivered remotely through Infinited Art.

3How can I collaborate with your team?

You can reach out to us via our contact page or directly through our subsidiary websites. We welcome partnerships in education, retail, and design sectors.

1What are the course fees at Canies Academy?

Canies Academy offers both free and paid learning programs. Pilot workshops cost as low as ₹50, while blooming courses start at ₹999 per month.

2Does Bitoramart charge for delivery?

Bitoramart offers free delivery for orders above ₹499. Below that, a nominal fee is charged based on distance and order type.

3 Is there a pricing plan for interior design services?

Yes, Infinited Art offers customized interior development packages based on your space, style, and budget. From home makeovers to workspace setups, pricing is tailored after a consultation.

1How can I contact your support team?

You can fill out our contact form, email us, or call the support numbers listed on each brand’s website. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

2Do you offer post-purchase support?

Absolutely! Whether it's LMS access for students or order tracking for deliveries, we ensure full support through calls, WhatsApp, and emails.

3Is live support available?

Live support is available during working hours (10 AM – 7 PM IST). Chatbots are also integrated for FAQs on our Canies Academy and Bitoramart platforms.
